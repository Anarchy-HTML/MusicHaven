About Us

Our Story

In April 1st, 2024, I, Anarchy, made Music Haven. Back then, it was themed after New Zealand, and was... well, there's no word other than dead. A few days after making Music Haven, I joined a server called "Rolism Royale", though it wouldn't get that name until months later. I liked the community a lot, and had never really settled into a server like this one.

However, as with all good things, there was a catch. while I liked some of the members quite well, namely Mir, Nameless, Creepy, and Dizzy, some of the staff were slightly... unfit (not to point fingers but- Crouton). In the end, I don't hold anything against these members, and they all had their own issues and reasons for taking out their stress on other members.

Unfortunately, by August, this server was starting to become something of a chore. I had become a member of the staff team (Event Manager), and, within two weeks of starting, I had been demoted for "not making events". I won't get into the details, but this was not at all true. One of the main reasons I left Rolism Royale was because the owner, Dizzy, got incredibly bossy and lost his temper easily.

Since I had joined his server, I had been striving to make something as close as possible to Rolism Royale, but- I never thought to make it better.

Us Now

We are currently working hard to achive our goals and keep this a fun, active, welcoming community. I speak for the entire staff team when I say that this is a nice server. Don't believe me?

Our future

We strive to be everything Rolism Royale couldn't be, as well as everything it was. We want to be an active, welcoming community and gain 300+ members, without disrespecting or mistreating the staff.
